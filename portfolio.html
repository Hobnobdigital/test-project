Case Study: Scaling a Fintech App to $10M ARR

The Challenge

A Series B fintech startup needed to scale paid acquisition from $500K/month to $2M/month while maintaining CAC below $150 and improving LTV:CAC ratio to 3:1+ for sustainable unit economics.

The Approach

  • Channel Expansion: Diversified from Meta-only to Google, TikTok, Reddit, reducing platform risk
  • Creative Engine: Built UGC program with 20+ creators, producing 150+ ad variants per month

  • Incrementality Testing: Ran geo-holdout tests, cutting non-performing spend by 30%
  • Cross-Functional Alignment: Partnered with Product to improve onboarding, lifting activation by 18%

The Outcome

Within 12 months, we scaled to $1.8M/month in paid spend with a blended CAC of $132 (12% below target), LTV:CAC of 3.4:1, and contributed directly to reaching $10M ARR ‚Äî 6 months ahead of plan.
